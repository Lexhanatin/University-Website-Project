Healers Medical Center

Healers health services comprises several hospitals, health centres, a world-class medical research institute, and pathology and pharmacy businesses—all with one aim—to provide Exceptional Care. Our concerted pursuit of innovation—to discover, improve, adopt and adapt—differentiates Healers as a leader in the areas of health, education and research. By integrating these fields into the delivery of exceptional healthcare services, Healers is committed to the development and maintenance of healthy communities.

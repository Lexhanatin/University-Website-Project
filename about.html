About Us

General Information

Healers health services comprises several hospitals, health centres, a world-class medical research institute, and pathology and pharmacy businesses—all with one aim—to provide Exceptional Care. Our concerted pursuit of innovation—to discover, improve, adopt and adapt—differentiates Healers as a leader in the areas of health, education and research. By integrating these fields into the delivery of exceptional healthcare services, Healers is committed to the development and maintenance of healthy communities.

History

Healers was founded in 2001 and was the start of a tradition of care and compassion for the sick and needy. Healer's board of directors and staff have since upheld the philosophy and Mission of offering this care according to our core Values of Mercy, Dignity, Care, Commitment and Quality. Healers is able to meet the needs of the community by reinvesting any revenue from the business back into the provision of health care services. Healers is dedicated to providing healthcare services through a sincere commitment to our core values of Dignity, Care, Commitment and Quality.
